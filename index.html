Oda Nobunaga
Legacy Reign Rise

The Story of Japan's First Great Unifier

Rise

Through innovative use of weapons and tactics, Oda Nobunaga gradually grew in reputation and renown from relative obscurity to one of the most well respected, feared and influential rulers in Japanese history renown which would draw samurai and warriors to him, eventually forming one of the largest and most.

Reign

Following his initial victories and rise to power, Oda Nobunaga went on to become 'The First Great Unifier of Japan', conquering most of Honshu island, often with brutal ruthlessness which came to define much of his rule.

Legacy

Oda Nobunaga left a legacy of violence and conquest, but also innovation and progress in a highly class-ruled society. Oda Nobunaga's reign paved the way for his sucessors to conquer all Japan and end the fighting between Daimyo, warlords, a critical historical acheivement and the foundation for modern Japan as we know it today.
